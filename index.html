    • Final Assignment Part One:

  • 1. How would YOU define AI?
    Ans : Artificial intelligence (AI) is the ability of a computer or a robot controlled by a computer to do tasks that are usually done by humans because they require human intelligence and discernment.

  • 2. Explain an application or use-case of AI that fascinates YOU.
    Ans : Spam Filters, Mobile Check Deposits, Robot-readers

  • 3. Pick a specific industry or an aspect of our lives or society and describe how YOU think it will be impacted by Artificial Intelligence in future.
    Ans : Artificial intelligence is impacting the future of virtually every industry and every human being. Artificial intelligence has acted as the main driver of emerging technologies like big data, robotics and IoT, and it will continue to act as a technological innovator for the foreseeable future. PwC estimates that “artificial intelligence technologies could increase global GDP by $15.7 trillion, a full 14 percent, by 2030.” A 2017 study from Redwood Software and Sapio Research concluded that 60 percent of businesses can be automated in the next five years.
